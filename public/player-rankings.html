Rating System

Players start with a base rating of 1500. Ratings are updated after each match based on the result and the rating difference between opponents.

  • Win against higher rated player: +25 to +40
  • Win against similar rated player: +15 to +25
  • Win against lower rated player: +5 to +15
  • Losses result in corresponding rating decreases

Rankings Update

Rankings are updated automatically after each match. Tournament performance has a higher impact on rating changes.

  • Tournament matches: 1.5x rating impact
  • Tournament finals: 2x rating impact
  • Inactive players (no matches in 30 days) may see rating decay
